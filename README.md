# MeritSync
### AI-Assisted Continuous Performance Management Platform

> **Continuous Insights. Confident Decisions.**

MeritSync is an enterprise-grade performance management platform developed for **HPCL** to transform traditional annual appraisals into a **continuous, evidence-based, transparent, and fair evaluation process**.

Instead of relying on year-end memory and subjective judgments, MeritSync captures meaningful performance events throughout the year, validates them with evidence and stakeholder feedback, and assists managers with intelligent insights to support fair appraisal decisions.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional appraisal systems often suffer from:

- Subjective evaluations and managerial bias
- Recency bias during annual reviews
- Bell curve limitations
- Lack of documented evidence
- Limited transparency and employee trust

MeritSync addresses these challenges through continuous performance tracking and evidence-backed decision-making.

---

## Key Features

- **Performance Index (PI):** Dynamic performance score based on achievements, stakeholder validation, consistency, leadership, learning, and integrity.
- **Continuous Performance Logging:** Capture significant positive and negative performance events throughout the year.
- **Evidence-Based Validation:** Every important event is supported by documents, emails, SAP reports, or approvals.
- **360° Validation Workflow:** Performance events are verified by Reporting Officers, Reviewing Officers, Internal Customers, and other stakeholders.
- **Performance Insights:** Analyze competencies, identify trends, summarize achievements, and provide explainable recommendations.
- **Bias Detection:** Detect recency bias, favoritism, leniency, and unusual rating patterns.
- **Immutable Performance Ledger:** Maintain secure, tamper-proof, and auditable records of all validated events.
- **Bell Curve Analytics:** Compare actual performance with rating distributions for informed appraisal decisions.
- **Automated Appraisal Reports:** Generate evidence-backed performance summaries and appraisal reports.
